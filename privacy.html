Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Teleprompter ("the App") is a free teleprompter application. This policy explains what data the App handles.

Data we collect

The App collects no personal data and sends nothing to any server. All information you enter (scripts, settings) is stored only in your browser's local storage on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

Camera and microphone

If you choose to use the optional video recording feature, the App requests access to your camera and microphone. The recorded video is saved directly to your device. No video or audio is uploaded or shared.

Third-party services

The App is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser, request time) as described in GitHub's Privacy Statement. The App itself does not add any additional tracking.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue at the project repository on GitHub.
